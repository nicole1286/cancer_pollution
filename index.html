Lung Cancer & Air Pollution

Question: Are different air pollutants related to cancer rates per state? Which ones have a bigger influence if any at all?

Hypothesis: We believe that air pollutants will increase cancer rates. We think carbon monoxide will have the strongest impact.

Method: Compare & analyze 2 data sets (air pollution & cancer) then train our model with our relevant data and test our predictions. We will clean data with pandas and create interactive visualizations with plotly & tableau. The graphs might help to answer our questions regarding the relationship between pollutants and cancer rates.

Sources: US Pollution Data from Kaggle, incidence rate from statecancerprofiles.cancer.gov

Machine Learning Steps

  • Assign variables X (data) and y (target).
  • Split data into training/testing.
  • Import machine learning libraries.
  • Create scaler models and fit data.
  • Transform train/test data using scalers.
  • Create linear Regression model and fit to scaled data.
  • Make predictions with X test scaled data.
  • Plot the train vs test data for comparison of residuals.

Residual Plot
